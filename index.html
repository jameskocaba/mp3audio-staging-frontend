    About MP3aud.io

    MP3aud.io was originally developed as a specialized utility for digital audio preservation, ensuring your favorite media is securely archived offline. Today, it has evolved into a comprehensive media processing suite. Alongside high-fidelity MP3 extraction, we now offer advanced AI-powered transcription and formatting.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. How do I back up my media to MP3 or generate notes?

    Simply copy the shareable URL, paste it into the search box, set any optional start/end times via Advanced Options, toggle "Generate Smart Notes" if desired, and click "Process". Our system will process the link and generate a ZIP file.

    2. What does the "Generate Smart Notes" toggle do?

    When activated, our backend uses advanced AI to transcribe the audio file. It then intelligently detects if the audio is a "DIY/Instructional Video" or a "Professional Meeting" and generates formatted HTML notes. Please note: This feature is currently compatible with only some social media video URLs.

    3. How do the Start and End time fields work?

    If you only want a specific segment of an audio file, you can input a Start Time (e.g., 01:20) and an End Time (e.g., 05:45) in the Advanced Options. Our processor will clip the audio to those exact timestamps.

    4. What audio quality are the MP3 backups?

    We prioritize audio fidelity. Our tool attempts to capture the highest available bitrate provided by the shareable link (typically 128kbps).

    5. How many files can I convert at once?

    You can process up to 50 files per playlist URL. Files are processed sequentially to ensure maximum reliability.

    6. Do I need to keep the browser tab open?

    If you are signed into an account, you do not need to keep the tab open. We will automatically email you a secure download link once your ZIP file is ready. Note: This link automatically expires after 1 hour.

    7. How long will my conversion take?

    Because processing high-quality audio and running AI transcription is resource-intensive, conversions are handled in a queue. If the server is busy, you will be given a queue position and an estimated wait time.

    Privacy Policy

    We prioritize your privacy. We do not store user data or IP logs, and all generated files are permanently deleted from our servers shortly after processing.

    Data Collection & Retention:

    • Email Addresses: If provided, emails are used strictly by our email provider to send a one-time "Download Ready" notification. They are not stored permanently or sold.
    • Your Files: Converted MP3s, ZIP archives, and generated text files are temporarily stored on our server to allow you to download them. They are automatically and permanently deleted 1 hour after the last update to your session.

    Third-Party AI Processing:

    • If you choose to use the AI Transcription feature, your audio files are temporarily securely transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate transcripts and summaries. OpenAI does not use data submitted via their API to train their models.

    Cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand site usage and improve our service. You can opt out using browser settings or extensions.
