    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. How do I process my local audio files?

    Simply drag and drop your music files or folders into the dropzone, select your desired enhancements (like Attach Lyrics or Auto-Add Album Art & Tags), and click "Process". Our system will process the files and generate an organized ZIP file.

    2. What does the "Attach Lyrics" toggle do?

    When activated, our backend uses advanced AI to physically listen to the audio track and transcribe the lyrics. It then takes those lyrics and physically embeds them into the metadata tag of your final audio file so it shows up in media players!

    3. Will this tool convert my files to MP3?

    No! By default, this tool preserves your original file format (e.g., WAV stays WAV). However, if you explicitly toggle "Increase Bitrate (320k)", the system will upsample your audio and convert it to a standard high-fidelity MP3.

    4. How many files can I process at once?

    You can always process up to 5 tracks completely for free. Additionally, with a $5.00 cryptocurrency payment, you can process up to 350 files per ZIP upload. Paid users get top priority and skip the server queue.

    5. Do I need to keep the browser tab open?

    If you are signed into an account, you do not need to keep the tab open. We will automatically email you a secure download link once your organized ZIP file is ready. Note: This link automatically expires after 1 hour.

    6. How long will my conversion take?

    Because processing high-quality audio and transcribing lyrics is resource-intensive, jobs are handled in a queue. If the server is busy, you will be given a queue position and an estimated wait time. Premium Priority: Jobs processed using paid credits automatically skip ahead of free users in the queue.

    Privacy Policy

    We prioritize your privacy. We do not store user data or IP logs, and all generated files are permanently deleted from our servers shortly after processing.

    Data Collection & Retention:

    • Email Addresses: If provided, emails are used strictly by our email provider to send a one-time "Download Ready" notification. They are not stored permanently or sold.
    • Your Files: Converted MP3s, ZIP archives, and generated text files are temporarily stored on our server to allow you to download them. They are automatically and permanently deleted 1 hour after the last update to your session.

    Third-Party AI Processing:

    • If you choose to use the AI Transcription feature, your audio files are temporarily securely transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate transcripts and summaries. OpenAI does not use data submitted via their API to train their models.

    Cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand site usage and improve our service. You can opt out using browser settings or extensions.
